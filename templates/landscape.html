Landscape

DESIGN/BUILD/MAINTAIN

Landscape improvements can make a tremendous difference in the looks, usefulness and value of your property. No matter what the style or size of your house, your property or your budget, our landscape designers can offer a variety of creative ideas to fit your needs. At Wesley’s Landscape & Lawncare, we ask how you want to use your property and then help you create both your short- and long-term plans.

From the smallest to the largest jobs, we start with the basics of good design: combining function with form, and beauty with utility. Our knowledgeable and experienced professionals will work with you from concept to project completion, with services that include a custom drawing of your project, a detailed proposal and a schedule that works for you. We like to think of it as an “Investment that Grows.”

Spring & Fall Clean-Up

Wesley’s Landscape & Lawncare offers clean-up services twice a year to help keep your lawn and landscape looking their best at all times. Our spring & fall clean-ups leave all beds spotless with weeding, general clean-up of leaves and debris, deadheading perennials and cutting back ornamental grasses.
